Self Tape May Engagement Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data collected through our application. Our web application aggregates publicly available information from Instagram for public use. We respect user privacy and aim to operate transparently.

2. Data Collection

Our application does not collect any personal information that is not already available through Instagram. Instead, we access and aggregate publicly available data from Instagram, such as posts, hashtags, and profile information, without altering or supplementing the original content not already provided by the user at www.selftapemay.com.

3. Data Use

The data we aggregate is used solely for the purposes of:

  • Facilitating the Self Tape May participation
  • Analyzing trends based on current and historical public data, such as hashtag usage or popular posts, for the benefit of improving and understanding Self Tape May

We do not modify, claim ownership of, or sell the data collected.

4. Data Sharing

Since we aggregate only publicly available data, we do not share, sell, or distribute this information beyond displaying it within our app, or at https://www.selftapemay.com. Any insights or summaries are based solely on public data and are accessible to all users.

5. User Privacy

While we work with public data, we respect the original content rights of Instagram users. Our app does not store private or personal data that is not explicitly accessible on Instagram to all users. Unless said data has been provided by the user voluntarily through www.selftapemay.com.

6. Compliance with Instagram's Terms

Self Tape May Engagement app adheres to Instagram's terms and community guidelines. Should Instagram users wish to restrict access to their content, they may change their privacy settings on Instagram to private. Our app will not be able to display or access data from private accounts.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect any changes in our practices or relevant laws. Users will be notified of any material changes via a notice on our application.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at wes@data2insightsllc.com.
